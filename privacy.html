Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 08, 2026

ChemCalc Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by ChemCalc Pro.

ChemCalc Pro collects minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email address and profile via Supabase.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous information about how you interact with the app.
  • Advertisement Data: We use Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers for personalized advertising.

2. Use of Data

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service.

  • To monitor the usage of our Service and detect technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools that have their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase: For database and authentication services.
  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store: For processing payments and subscriptions.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via the feedback section in the app or at support@chemcalcpro.com.
